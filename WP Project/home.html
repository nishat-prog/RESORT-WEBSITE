An Immortal Palace By Sea

Welcome To Kaldan Samudhra

Best hotels and resorts in Mahabalipuram

A marvelous home away from home. This sprawling 10 acre palatial property embodies the very picture of eminence and nobility that celebrates the resplendence of an age where royalty
was revered and celebrated. Our artisans have created a palace that is an extravagant and luxurious amalgamation of Rajasthani architecture and palatial arts, exuding divine elegance
and sophistication and bringing forth an experience of immortality.
















Kaldan is located on the beautiful shores of Bay of Bengal, 2 Kms before the Temple city of Mahabalipuram. With its 106 luxurious rooms, suites and villas Kaldan is ready to welcome
and treat you as Kings and Queens of the Palace.

With largest swimming pool, sprawling infinite lawns merging into the private beach, curated unique experiences and global choice of cuisines, Kaldan has something to offer to
ensure your well-earned holiday is a memorable one with your friends and families.
