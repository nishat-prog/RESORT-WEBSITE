About Us





Our Story



Kaldan means an immortal place in Paradise, and we as Brand Ambassadors, we create lifestyle and luxury experiences for our discerning Guests which will remain immortal in their memories. Our Palace is done with an amalgamation of Rajasthani architecture and palatial arts, started construction in 2017 and brought to life on 9th December 2021 by skilled artisans, sculptors and builders from all over India.


Famous for the intricate architecture of its historical forts, Rajasthan has long been the home to kings and queens. To its east, Agra too has housed the greatest royal love story in the Taj Mahal. And if you go further south, you’ll stumble across India’s favourite beach destination, Goa. Thousands of kilometers and days’ worth of travel separate these Northern and Southern cities. But Kaldan Samudhra offers you the opportunity to experience the best of these geo-cultures under one magnificent roof.

The word ‘Kaldan’ symbolises immortality in paradise. The brand’s vision is to create an eternal luxury experience for its discerning guests. Sprawling over 10 acres, the palace hotel stands tall as a beautiful reminder of the Taj Mahal. Reimagining the best of ancient Rajasthani architecture and palatial arts, the beachfront palace boasts of 106 luxurious rooms, suites, and sea-facing villas with private beach access. Located on the East Coast Road overlooking the Bay of Bengal, the property shares the same air as the majestic Shore Temple in Mahabalipuram.

Your leisure holiday begins with a traditional Rajasthani welcome waiting to greet you at the doorstep. You can also make the most of palatial experiences and cultural affairs such as their palace high tea, Rajasthani folk performances, and sunrise yoga sessions by the beach. Hospitality and service is at the heart of Kaldan Samudhra’s philosophy, which borrows from the traditions of ancient kingdoms. Much like the kings and queens of bygone eras, the staff prides itself in making you feel like royalty throughout your stay.

Whether you’re planning a lavish weekend getaway or a grand wedding, your visit to Kaldan Samudhra will remain immortal in your memory. After all, where else can you find a palace fit for royalty with a splendid, sea-facing view?
